Cataract Surgery

Micro-incision Phacoemulsification (MICS)

Advanced stitchless cataract surgery using the latest phacoemulsification technology. We offer premium IOL options including multifocal, toric, and EDOF lenses for optimal vision correction tailored to your lifestyle needs in Anantapur.

Treatment Journey

Pre-operative Assessment

Comprehensive eye exam, biometry for IOL power calculation, corneal topography, and medical clearance (1-2 hours)

Surgery Day

Micro-incision phacoemulsification with premium IOL implantation (15-20 minutes, daycare procedure)

Immediate Recovery

Rest for 2-4 hours at clinic, protective shield provided, medications prescribed

Follow-up Care

Day 1, Week 1, and Month 1 post-op visits for monitoring and visual rehabilitation

Cornea & Corneal Transplantation

Advanced Corneal Surgery & Expert Care

Specialized corneal disease management and transplantation services. Our experienced corneal specialist provides comprehensive care for corneal infections, dystrophies, and requires transplantation. We offer all modern techniques including penetrating keratoplasty, lamellar keratoplasty (DALK, DSEK, DMEK), and amniotic membrane transplantation - positioning cornea care as a hero specialty of our clinic.

When to Consult Our Cornea Specialist

Multiple symptoms detected. Schedule an immediate consultation.

Corneal Transplantation Procedures:
Penetrating Keratoplasty (PKP)

Full-thickness corneal transplant for severe scarring, keratoconus, and endothelial dysfunction. Success rate >90% with proper donor-recipient matching and post-operative management.

Deep Anterior Lamellar Keratoplasty (DALK)

Partial-thickness transplant preserving patient's endothelium. Ideal for keratoconus and superficial corneal opacity. Lower rejection rate than PKP with faster visual recovery.

Descemet Stripping Endothelial Keratoplasty (DSEK)

Endothelial transplant for Fuchs dystrophy and bullous keratopathy. Minimal astigmatism, faster visual rehabilitation than PKP, excellent long-term endothelial cell survival.

Descemet Membrane Endothelial Keratoplasty (DMEK)

State-of-the-art endothelial transplant. Most selective procedure with highest endothelial cell retention. Superior visual acuity outcomes, minimal astigmatism. Requires specialized equipment and expertise.

Amniotic Membrane Transplantation (AMT)

Biologic dressing for severe dry eye, Stevens-Johnson Syndrome, chemical burns, and corneal erosions. Promotes healing, reduces inflammation and pain. Can be used standalone or adjunctive to other procedures.

Corneal Infection Treatment:
  • •Bacterial Keratitis: Fortified antibiotics, corneal culture, confocal microscopy for diagnosis and monitoring
  • •Fungal Keratitis: Antifungal therapy, therapeutic debridement, and corneal transplant when necessary
  • •Viral Keratitis: Antiviral medications, management of post-herpetic scarring and neovascularization
  • •Acanthamoeba Keratitis: Specialized diagnostic protocols and aggressive therapy to prevent vision loss
Important: Early diagnosis and treatment of corneal infections are critical to prevent permanent vision loss. Any eye pain, redness, or vision changes require immediate evaluation.

Glaucoma Management

The Silent Thief of Sight - Early Detection

Comprehensive glaucoma screening and management, crucial for the high diabetic population in Andhra Pradesh. Our advanced diagnostic tools detect glaucoma before vision loss occurs, with personalized treatment plans including medication, laser therapy, and surgical options.

Are You at Risk?

  • • Age above 40 years
  • • Family history of glaucoma
  • • Diabetes or hypertension
  • • High myopia (nearsightedness)
  • • Previous eye injury

Check Your Symptoms

Multiple symptoms detected. Schedule an immediate consultation.

Diagnostic Tests:
  • • Intraocular Pressure (IOP) Measurement
  • • Visual Field Analysis (Perimetry)
  • • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)
  • • Gonioscopy for angle assessment
  • • Pachymetry (corneal thickness)
Treatment Modalities:
  • • Medicated Eye Drops (first line)
  • • YAG Laser Peripheral Iridotomy
  • • Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT)
  • • Glaucoma Filtration Surgery
  • • Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgery (MIGS)

Retinal Care

Diabetic Retinopathy & Macular Disorders

Specialized treatment for diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration, and retinal detachments. Given that Andhra Pradesh has one of India's highest diabetes rates, our retina clinic is equipped with advanced laser and injection therapies to preserve your vision.

Regional Health Alert: Andhra Pradesh

Nearly 45% of diabetic patients in India present with vision loss at first eye care visit. With diabetes prevalence rising in Anantapur, annual retinal screening is crucial for all diabetics over 30.

LASIK & Refractive Surgery

Freedom from Glasses

Advanced laser vision correction using state-of-the-art excimer laser technology. We offer customized LASIK, PRK, and SMILE procedures to correct myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism with rapid recovery and excellent visual outcomes.

Are You a Candidate?

  • ✓ Age 18-40 years with stable prescription
  • ✓ No active eye diseases or infections
  • ✓ Adequate corneal thickness
  • ✓ Not pregnant or nursing
  • ✓ Realistic expectations
The LASIK Process:
  1. Comprehensive pre-LASIK evaluation (corneal topography, pachymetry, tear film analysis)
  2. Creation of corneal flap using microkeratome or femtosecond laser
  3. Excimer laser reshaping of cornea (30-60 seconds per eye)
  4. Flap repositioning - no stitches required
Recovery Timeline:
  • • Day 1: Rest, protective shield, blurred vision
  • • Day 2-3: Most patients achieve 6/9 to 6/6 vision
  • • Week 1: Return to normal activities, no swimming
  • • Month 1: Vision stabilization complete

Pediatric Eye Care

Child-Friendly Environment

Specialized eye care for infants, children, and adolescents. Early detection and treatment of refractive errors, squint (strabismus), lazy eye (amblyopia), and congenital cataracts ensure optimal visual development for your child's future.

Recommended Screening Schedule

Newborn: Red reflex test, structural abnormalities
6-12 months: Alignment check, fix and follow
3-5 years: Visual acuity, squint assessment
School age: Annual comprehensive exam

Warning Signs in Children

• Sitting too close to TV
• Squinting or head tilting
• Frequent eye rubbing
• Avoiding reading
• Misaligned eyes
• White pupil reflex
Conditions Treated:
  • • Refractive errors (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism)
  • • Amblyopia (Lazy Eye) therapy
  • • Strabismus (Squint) - surgical & non-surgical
  • • Congenital cataracts and glaucoma
  • • Pediatric eye infections and allergies
Specialized Tests:
  • • Cycloplegic refraction
  • • Squint evaluation (cover test, prism bars)
  • • Synoptophore for binocular vision
  • • OCT for pediatric retina
  • • Examination under anesthesia when needed
